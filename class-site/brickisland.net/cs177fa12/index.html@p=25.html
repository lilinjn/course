    Looks like a form of the Kalman filter. Here is a formula for the Fenchel conjugate $f^*$ of a real-valued function $f$ (in physics, a Fenchel conjugate is also known as a Legendre transform):

    $f^*(y) = \sup_{x} \left( y^T x – f(x) \right)$

    $f^*$ is always convex, even if $f$ is not.
